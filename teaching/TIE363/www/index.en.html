Mailing lists

The course has a formal mailing list for informing which has a public archive. Weekly contemplation are shared with others in discussion mailing list [ archive].

Manner of Performance

To pass the course a small software project in course's subject matter is accomplished. Weekly contemplation make it possible to have a higher grade. Additional information for manner of performance.

Accomplished software projects are presented in informal seminar which operates as a last lecture for the course.

Grading

50% of the grade will be for weekly contemplation and the rest 50% for software project. Therefore, with excellent project you are passed with grade one. The word excellent doesn't mean here scaling between students but stands for personal show and the process of learning trough the course. In practise everyone who participates in project will pass so don't panic before hand.
