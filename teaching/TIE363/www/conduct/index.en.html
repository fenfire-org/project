Manner of Performance

To pass the course a small software project in course's subject matter is accomplished. Weekly contemplation make it possible to have a higher grade.

Weekly Contemplation

Weekly contemplation are sent to public mailing list for them [ archive]. These contemplation are appropriate for different people and consist of following methods:

  • General discussion
  • Short essay
  • Programming practisement

If you feel pressures for sending your contemplation to mailing list and this makes it hard to pass the course, please contact lecturers. We propose you to filter "tie363" emails in different folder than INBOX. There's also short help to set procmail in here.

Everyone is encouraged to participate in discussions, and that way raise your grade. The ideal situation would be that you don't even feel like studing but pleasurable sharing of contemplation.

General Discussion

A student participates to mailing list discussion. Discussions can vary around the given contemplation, a lecture or somehthing else which someway affects the subject. You shall write out different point of views and critique is welcome as well (thank you). Lecturers give feedback acording to your mails and thus everyone has an opornity to envolve in mailing list discussion.

Mailing list are well known method to discuss between free software developers. As an example you may look lkml.

Essay

A student can contemplate with essay as well. The essay doesn't have to be long, from three to six paragraphs is fine. Of course even a longer ones are welcome too. By the way, your essay may arise general discussion in mailing list and by defending your opinion you can collect bonus points.

Writing is a very valuable skill and therefore a student has an opornity to participate to weekly contemplation by essays too. Essay writing is warmly recommended for all. Nerd have to be able to document the code. Now it's a good time to have a practise in writing.

Programming Practisement

You can also participate to contemplation by programming few hours. The language of your choice has to be free (Java is an exception). You need to remember to document your code, tell how does the program work and add your copyright notice.

Software Project

Example subjects

Here it goes:

  • Port a program of your choice to use identity-technologies (to Fenfire framework).
  • Come up with your own idea for program which uses idendtity methods.
  • Port OpenGL part of Libvob to other operation system, e.g., OS-X or Windows.
  • An alternative system for IRC like chatting which can make use of identity objects, e.g., images, xanalogic media etc.
  • Debian/RPM/OsX/(Windows?) paketing.

Fenfire Source Code

You shall use Fenfire resources to accomplish your software project. Almoust every piece of code is freely available with five commands.

The best place for help with Fenfire source code is available from #fenfire IRC-channel which can be found from irc.freenode.net server. Everyone is welcome to join.
