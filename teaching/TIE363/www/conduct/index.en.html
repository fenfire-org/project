Manner of Performance

To pass the course a small software project in course's subject matter is accomplished. Weekly contemplation make it possible to have a higher grade.

Weekly Contemplation

Weekly contemplation is sent to public mailing list [ archive]. Contemplation is appropriate for different people and consist of following methods:

  • General discussion
  • Short essay
  • Programming practise

If you feel pressure for sending your contemplation to mailing list and this makes it hard to pass the course, please contact the lecturers. We recommend filtering "tie363" emails in different folder than INBOX. There's also short help to set procmail in here.

Everyone is encouraged to participate in discussion, and that way raise your grade. The ideal situation would be that it will not even feel like studing but pleasurable sharing of ideas.

General Discussion

A student participates mailing list discussion, which can vary around the given contemplation, a lecture or somehthing else which some way affects the subject. You shall write out different points of views and critique is welcome as well (thank you). Lecturers give feedback according to your mails and thus everyone has an oppornity to get involved in mailing list discussion.

Mailing list are well-known method for discussion between free software developers. As an example you may look lkml.

Essay

A student can contemplate with essays as well. The essay doesn't have to be long, three to six paragraphs is fine; longer ones are welcome too. By the way, your essay may arouse general discussion in mailing list and by defending your opinion you can collect bonus points.

Writing is a very valuable skill and therefore a student has an oppornity to participate in weekly contemplation by essays too. Essay writing is warmly recommended for all. Consider it an opportunity to have a practise in writing.

Programming Practise

You can also participate by a programming task of varying weight. The language of your choice has to be free (Java is an exception). You need to remember to document your code, tell how the program works and add your copyright notice.

Software Project

Example subjects

  • Port a program of your choice to use identity-technologies (to Fenfire framework).
  • Come up with an idea for program which uses identity methods.
  • Port the OpenGL part of Libvob to another operation system, e.g., OS-X or Windows.
  • An alternative system for IRC-like chatting which can make use of identity objects, e.g., images, xanalogic media etc.
  • Debian/RPM/OsX/(Windows?) packaging.

Fenfire Source Code

You shall use Fenfire resources to accomplish your software project. Almost every piece of code is freely available in five commands.

The best place to get help with the Fenfire source code is the #fenfire IRC-channel, which is located in the Freenode IRC Network (irc.freenode.net) Everyone is welcome to join.
